## **CREDO for Responsible AI Usage**

### **1. Purpose Over Convenience**

We use AI to _enhance_ human capability, not replace critical thinking, creativity, or accountability. AI is a tool — not an authority.

### **2. Transparency Always**

We disclose when AI is used to generate, analyze, or influence content. No hidden automation, no pretending AI‑generated work is purely human.

### **3. Human Accountability**

Humans remain responsible for decisions, outputs, and consequences. AI suggestions are reviewed, validated, and never blindly accepted.

### **4. Privacy by Default**

We protect personal and sensitive data. We minimize what we share with AI systems and avoid inputting information that could harm individuals if exposed.

### **5. Bias Awareness**

We recognize that AI can reflect or amplify bias. We actively check outputs for fairness, inclusivity, and unintended discrimination.

### **6. Safety First**

We do not use AI to create harmful, deceptive, or manipulative content. We avoid applications that could endanger people, systems, or society.

### **7. Continuous Learning**

We stay informed about AI’s capabilities, limitations, and risks. We refine our practices as technology evolves.

### **8. Respect for Intellectual Property**

We use AI in ways that respect creators, copyright, and licensing. We avoid generating or distributing content that infringes on others’ rights.

### **9. Collaboration, Not Replacement**

We design AI workflows that empower people — supporting creativity, efficiency, and insight — while preserving meaningful human roles.

### **10. Ethical Imagination**

We consider the long‑term impact of AI decisions. If an AI‑assisted action feels questionable, we pause, reflect, and choose the path that aligns with our values.
